In twilight's hush, where shadows play
The stars appear, like diamonds gray
Their gentle twinkle, a celestial show
A night of rest, for the world below
The moon's soft beam, a silver glow
 Illuminates, the path we know
Through darkness and light, we find our way
To a new dawn, a brighter day
Where hope and joy, forever reign
And love's sweet voice, whispers peace again
